Along the River During the Qingming Festival

Solo Developer
April 2022 - April 2022

Along The River During The Qingming Festival is an interactive experience that uncovers the ancient Chinese namesake painting stroke by stroke. Audio cues such as crowd ambience, nature, and animals hint players to uncover different regions of the painting, all tied together with vibrant traditional Chinese music.
