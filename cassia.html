Cassia

Programmer
April 2018

Cassia is a 2D virtual helper designed to serve as an information kiosk for public events. Users interact with Cassia through a touch screen and voice commands.

Mission

After hosting multiple successful public events for Planet Save, I noticed that there was a disproportional ratio of visitors to crew members. That resulted in many visitors leaving our booth prematurely without ever having the chance to see and learn about our many environmental projects. Cassia is designed to remedy that issue by providing visitors an educational and entertaining interactive experience while they wait to interact with our team.

Technical

  • Built in Unity (C#)
  • Animations generated from one still image using Live2D
  • Voice recognition handled by Windows Speech API
